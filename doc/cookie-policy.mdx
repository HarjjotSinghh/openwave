# Cookie Policy

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when visiting websites. They help sites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

## 2. How We Use Cookies
- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for core functionality (login, payments)
- **Preference Cookies**: Remember your language and display settings
- **Analytics Cookies**: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
- **Marketing Cookies**: Show relevant project recommendations (opt-in)

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
- Google Analytics for usage statistics
- Stripe for payment processing
- GitHub for authentication AVAXs

## 4. Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy as our services evolve. Significant changes will be announced through platform notifications.
