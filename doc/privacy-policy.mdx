# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Information**: Name, email, GitHub username when you register
- **Payment Details**: Encrypted payment information for transactions
- **Project Data**: Information about projects you create or fund
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns

## 2. How We Use Information
- To provide and improve our services
- To process transactions and send notifications
- To personalize your experience and recommend relevant projects
- To communicate important service updates

## 3. Data Protection
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
- Encryption of sensitive data both in transit and at rest

## 4. Third-Party Services
- We use trusted payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Analytics services help us understand usage patterns
- We never sell your personal information to advertisers

## 5. Your Rights
- Access and download your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
